“Kugeza ubwo twese tuzagera ku bumwe mu kwemera no kumenya Umwana w’Imana, tukaba abantu bakuze bageze ku gipimo cy’ubuzure bwa Kristo.”
– Abefeso 4:13 (Bibiliya Yera 2023)

Uko Bikorwa:

  • Wipime kandi utibereye hagati ya 0 na 5 kuri buri gice cy’ubuzima bwawe bwo mu mwuka.
  • 0: Nta ntambwe, 5 = Imbaraga zihamye kandi zigaragara.
  • 1: Byanze kugaragara / Haracyakenewe gukura cyane.
  • 2: Bigaragara rimwe na rimwe / Biri gukura buhoro.
  • 3: Biri hagati / Hari aho gigenda neza rimwe narimwe.
  • 4: Bigaragara kenshi / Hari imbaraga mu bice byinshi.
  • 5: Bihoraho kandi byuzuye / Ndakomeye cyane.
  • Ukimara kuzuza urahita ubona amanota yose n’ijanisha ry’aho ugeze mu rugendo rwo gukura mu mwuka.
  • Kanda kuri "Bika kopi(Print)" kugira ngo ubone kopi yuzuye.
